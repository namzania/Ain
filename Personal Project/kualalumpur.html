KUALA LUMPUR


MANDARIN ORIENTAL



Mandarin Oriental, Kuala Lumpur has an enviable location - tucked between the lush green KLCC Park and the striking, 452m-Petronas Twin Towers - the tallest pair of towers in the world. Step out of the hotel and you ar immediately part of the buzzing metropolis.Whether you are keen on sipping cocktails, poolside lounging, or high-end dining, here's where you can do all that while savouring the best of Kuala Lumpur's skyline.

There's no better location to relax and see the sights simultaneously than at Mandarin Oriental,Kuala Lumpur's infinity pool. Take in the impressive skyline as you paddle, of if you prefer not to get wet, relax at one of the shaded poolside cabanas with a frozen margarita. For a post-pool massage, take solace at The Spa, where the Digital Wellness Escape focuses on strains resulting from the frequent use of digital devices.
