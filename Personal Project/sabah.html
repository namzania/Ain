SHANGRI-LA RASA RIA RESORT & SPA



Shangri_la's Rasa Ria Reort & Spa, Kota Kinabalu on Pantai Dalit Beach. Set amidst a nature reserve, the resort enchants englessly with its natural magic. Your Malaysian-themed room in perched on a verdant hill, with a spectacular beachfront view, surrounded by 400 acres of tropical forest. You see the catamarans along the beach, and can only imagine what other activities await. The resort is home to an eco-friendly championship golf course and the magnificent Spa at Dalit Bay. Unique to the resort is the Discovery Centre, gateway to the Rasa Ria Reserve. Here, You can meet the Borneo's wildlife.

Even the kids have their own fun club. The more adventurous ones get to help the rangers look after the animals in the resort's care.
